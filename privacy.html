Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 31, 2026

1. Introduction

We take the protection of your personal data very seriously. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store and protect your personal information in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable laws.

2. Data Collected

We collect the following types of data:

  • Account Data: Name and email address. This information is stored on our servers only if you voluntarily create an account or sign up for updates.
  • Device & Technical Data: Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for analytics.
  • Usage Data: App interaction patterns, session duration, and crash reports to improve performance.
  • User inputs: All data entered into the functional parts of our Apps is processed locally on your device and is never stored on our servers.
  • Communication Data: Content of support requests and feedback sent via the app.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Your explicit consent
  • Contract execution
  • Compliance with a legal obligation
  • Protection of your vital interests or those of another person
  • Accomplishment of a public interest mission
  • Our legitimate interests

4. Use of Data

Your personal data is used for:

  • Providing our services and products
  • Processing your orders and payments
  • Responding to your requests and questions
  • Sending important communications about our service
  • Improving our mobile applications
  • Preventing fraud and ensuring security
  • Complying with our legal obligations

5. Data Sharing

We only share your personal data:

  • With authorized service providers who help us provide our services
  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights, your security or that of others
  • With your explicit consent

6. Cookies

Our site uses cookies to improve your user experience. Cookies are small files stored on your computer. You can configure your browser to refuse cookies, but some site features may not work properly.

Types of cookies used:

  • Session Cookies: Necessary for site functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand your site usage
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to personalize advertisements

7. Data Security

We will implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data against destruction, loss, alteration or unauthorized access. However, no data transmission over the Internet is completely secure.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services
  • Comply with our legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce our agreements

Once this period has passed, your data will be securely deleted or anonymized.

9. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access: Access your personal data
  • Correction: Correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction: Restrict processing of your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Opposition: Object to processing of your data

10. Children

Our site is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected data from a minor, we will delete it immediately.

11. Policy Changes

We may modify this privacy policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an update date. Your continued use of the site after changes means you accept the updated policy.
