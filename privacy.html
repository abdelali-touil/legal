Privacy Policy - Mobile Applications

Last updated: January 31, 2026

Important: This Privacy Policy applies to our mobile applications available on Google Play Store. By downloading and using our Apps, you agree to these terms. We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and Google Play Data Safety requirements.

1. What Data We Collect

When you use our mobile applications, we may collect:

  • Account Data: Name and email (only if you create an account)
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, and unique identifiers for analytics
  • Usage Data: App interactions, session duration, and crash reports to improve performance
  • App Permissions: Data from permissions you grant (camera, location, contacts, microphone, storage, etc.)
  • Local Data: Information you enter is processed only on your device and never stored on our servers
  • Support Data: Messages you send through support channels

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and improve our mobile applications
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Analyze app performance and user experience
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal requirements

3. Data Sharing

We only share your data with:

  • Service providers who help us operate our apps
  • Google Play Store (for distribution and compliance)
  • Law enforcement when legally required

We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. App Permissions

You have full control over app permissions:

  • Grant or deny permissions when the app requests them
  • Change permissions anytime through your device Settings
  • Denying permissions may limit app functionality
  • We only use permissions for their intended purpose

5. Data Security

We use appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data. However, no data transmission is completely secure.

6. Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as needed to provide services and comply with legal obligations. After that, we securely delete or anonymize it.

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR and CCPA, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data (right to be forgotten)
  • Restrict or object to data processing
  • Receive your data in a portable format

8. Age Requirements

Our apps are intended for users 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover such data, we will delete it immediately.

9. Policy Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated date. Your continued use of our apps means you accept the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

For privacy questions or to exercise your rights:

  • Support: Visit Support Center

11. Google Play and Legal Compliance

Our apps comply with Google Play Terms of Service, Google Play Policies, and Google Play Data Safety requirements.
